NFT Marketplace

This Solidity smart contract powers an NFT Marketplace on the Ethereum blockchain. It facilitates the creation, listing, and transactions of NFTs, providing users with diverse functionalities for engaging in the NFT ecosystem seamlessly.

Decentralised Portfolio

This Solidity smart contract enables individuals to curate and manage personal portfolios on the Ethereum blockchain. It facilitates the recording and display of project details, education, and work experience, providing users with a platform to showcase their achievements effectively.

Decentralized E-commerce

A Solidity smart contract for an Ethereum-based Ecommerce platform. This smart contract enables users to register products for sale, purchase products, confirm product delivery, and allows the manager to destroy the contract.
